Other New York City Boundaries

These are municipal boundaries that are determined by data from many sources and shaped to make sure all New Yorkers have fair access to City services like the Fire and Police Department. The US Government Census counts how many people live in not only New York, but the whole country. There are many more boundaries I could put on this map like voting districts, health areas, and zip codes, but that would get too crazy.

If you want to know how many of each of these areas there are in New York City, the number is next to the name on each button below.
